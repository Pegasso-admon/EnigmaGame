A dark chamber swallowed us whole. Only a massive screen glowed in front of us.

CRUDY appeared, her face shifting between static and code:

“In this trial, you will watch. Listen. Memorize. Patterns are everywhere.

Miss one… and you may not live to regret it.”

The screen blinked. Patterns appeared — sounds, shapes, glitches.

Then questions.

We hesitated.

One of us guessed.

His collar sparked… and his head slumped forward.

No time to mourn, the next pattern began.
