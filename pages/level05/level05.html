We entered a room of pure white. No walls. No shadows. Just a glowing console in the center…

Floating screens were forming a circle around us.

CRUDY appeared one last time... But different.

This time, no distortion. No flickers. Only a cold, human voice in a mechanical body.

“This is your Final Judgment. Here, logic alone won't save you. You must understand. You must think. You must adapt.”

The screens lit up with challenges:

Programming riddles Logical traps Error correction Nested structures Memory leaks Code obfuscation

And then CRUDY said:

"Each correct answer brings you closer to the exit. Each failure tightens your leash."

"A perfect score will set you free. A mediocre score… will bind you as one of my operators forever."

"But if you fail… if you fall short… your collar will seal your fate.""

The questions began. The ticking returned.

This time, you didn’t just play the game.

You became the code.
