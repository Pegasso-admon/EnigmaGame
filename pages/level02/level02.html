After surviving the horrors behind the door, we were dropped into a white, sterile chamber.

No shadows. No sounds. No warmth. Just a decaying wall ahead with a single, narrow passage in the middle.

You're not alone. Others stand, trembling, just as lost, just as trapped.

A maze.

We decided to stay together.We thought:

“It can’t get worse than this.”

We were wrong.

The maze branched into three paths. At the end of each, another door.

Familiar. But wrong.
